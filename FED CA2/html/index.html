Singapore Polytechnic

Singapore Polytechnic (SP) is the premier institution of polytechnic education in Singapore, renowned for its pioneering spirit and commitment to nurturing future-ready graduates. Our rich heritage, combined with a forward-thinking approach, empowers students to excel in a dynamic global landscape.

  • Established in 1954 as the first polytechnic in Singapore.
  • Over 15,000 students across 34 full-time diploma courses.
  • A vibrant campus with state-of-the-art facilities and resources.

Starting in a modest five-story building on Prince Edward Road, we have expanded into Singapore's largest polytechnic, now a vast campus covering 38 hectares of lush greenery with over 230,000 distinguished alumni. Our 70-year milestone in 2024 is not just a time for reflection and celebration but also the dawn of a new era, filled with opportunities for discovery and achievement. This is SP at 70.
