SP Open House 2024

SP Open House

Join us at the SP Open House to explore a landscape of opportunity and innovation. Our campus transforms into a dynamic showcase, featuring the very best of what SP has to offer to prospective students and their families.

  • Interactive Tours: Step into our advanced facilities where learning comes alive. From engineering workshops to digital media studios, see where your future begins.
  • Live Demonstrations: Witness real-time projects from current students, showcasing innovations in robotics, sustainable engineering, and digital arts.
  • Hands-On Workshops: Participate in sessions from programming to 3D printing, and get a real feel for the SP learning experience.
  • Student-Led Panels: Hear directly from SP students about their journeys, challenges, and accomplishments. Gain insights into student life and the vibrant community you could join.
SP Open House

This year, the SP campus came alive during our 70th Anniversary Open House. Visitors were captivated by immersive school tours showcasing our state-of-the-art facilities. At the Plaza, thrilling CCA performances added to the festive atmosphere. A highlight of the event was the remarkable drone swarming display, part of our inaugural SP Industry & Innovation Showcase, which left the audience in awe.

SP Music Festival

SP70th Anniversary Music Festival 2024

Celebrate 70 years of innovation and excellence with the SP70th Anniversary Music Festival 2024. Our goal is to create an unforgettable musical experience that reflects the vibrancy and diversity of Singapore Polytechnic's community. We are organizing a series of exciting events and performances to mark this milestone occasion.

  • Live Music Performances: Enjoy a variety of genres from talented student bands and renowned guest artists.
  • Cultural Showcases: Experience the rich tapestry of Singapore's music scene through traditional and contemporary performances.
  • Interactive Workshops: Participate in music production and songwriting sessions led by industry experts.
  • Art Installations: Explore creative displays that celebrate our journey and achievements over the past 70 years.
